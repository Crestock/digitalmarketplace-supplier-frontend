Dear supplier,

You, or one of your team, started an application to become a Digital Outcomes and Specialists 2 supplier. All contributors on your Digital Marketplace account will now receive email updates. The deadline for completing your application is 6 December 2016.

You must ask any clarification questions you have through your Digital Marketplace account before 5pm GMT, 22 November 2016.

To complete your application, you must make the supplier declaration, provide information about the services you offer, and mark each service as complete.

To understand if your services are suitable for Digital Outcomes and Specialists 2, you can read this blog post:
https://digitalmarketplace.blog.gov.uk/2015/11/12/digital-outcomes-and-specialists-suitable-suppliers-and-services

The Digital Outcomes and Specialists 2 application area of your Digital Marketplace account lets you:
  • find all the legal and guidance documentation
  • ask clarification questions
  • read responses to all supplier questions
  • find additional information to help you complete your application
Thanks,
The Digital Marketplace team
