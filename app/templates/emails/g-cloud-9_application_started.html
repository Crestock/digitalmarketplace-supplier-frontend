Dear supplier,

You, or one of your team, started an application to become a G-Cloud 9 supplier. All contributors on your Digital Marketplace account will now receive email updates. The deadline for completing your application is 5pm BST, 11 April 2017.

You must ask any clarification questions you have through your Digital Marketplace account before 5pm BST, 28 March 2017.

To complete your application, you must make the supplier declaration, provide information about the services you offer, and mark each service as complete.

To understand if your services are suitable for G-Cloud 9, read this guidance:
https://www.gov.uk/guidance/g-cloud-suppliers-guide

The G-Cloud 9 application area of your Digital Marketplace account lets you:
  • find all the legal and guidance documentation
  • ask clarification questions
  • read responses to all supplier questions
  • find additional information to help you complete your application
